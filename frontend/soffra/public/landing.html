SOFFRA:

Translated from the Italian, Soffra means to suffer. This is the visceral experience of a cyclist attempting to defy gravity by cycling uphill with nothing more than biology, physiology and momentum. The suffering is temporary, however, the experience and sense of accomplishment lives on in perpetuity.

APPLICATION FEATURES

View One-off, Gap, Pass Or Notch Climbs

Find one-off, gap, pass or notch climbs by searching any criteria that pertains to the climb such as grade, terrain distance or location.

Analyze Stats, Maps, Charts & Video

View terrain maps powered by Google, elevation and gradient charts, climbs statistics and video as well as details about the region, how to get there and everything else that you need to know to plan your next attack.

Visualize Climbs In Three Dimensions

Zoom in, rotate and fly around your favorite climb with 3D Terrain and Topographic maps built with ArcGIS mapping software.

How it Works

1

Get out and ride

Get out there and attack your favorite climb. We are adding new climbs everyday, so chances are, there is one or two in your neck of the woods.

2

Upload to Strava

Upload your ride to your Strava profile as normal. SOFFRA will detect the climbs that were completed and automatically update your profile climb checklist.

3

View your accomplishments

View your checklist, analyze your data & interact with athletes. You can also visit the individual climbs to view stats, maps, charts and video so you can plan your next attack.

4

Repeat

Climbing mountains on your bicycle is difficult but fun... go get some more!.
